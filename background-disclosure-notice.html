Background Disclosure and Authorization Notice

Progress Staffing Agency | Employee copy | Version 1 | March 2026

Progress Staffing Agency may obtain consumer reports and investigative consumer reports about you for employment purposes, to the extent permitted by applicable federal, state, and local law. This notice is provided so you can review the disclosure before you sign the electronic background acknowledgment and consent form in the employee portal.

By completing the portal consent form, you authorize lawful background screening related to hiring, assignment placement, reassignment, retention, and other employment-related decisions.

What May Be Reviewed

Depending on the role, client requirements, and applicable law, the screening process may include one or more of the following:

  • Identity verification
  • Criminal history searches
  • Employment history verification
  • Education verification
  • Professional license or certification checks
  • Motor vehicle records, where driving is job-related
  • Other job-related screening permitted by law

Your Rights

You may have rights under the Fair Credit Reporting Act and applicable state laws, including rights to receive additional disclosures, request information about the reporting agency used, dispute inaccurate information, and receive pre-adverse and adverse action notices when required by law.

Important Notes

  • This notice is intended to accompany the electronic consent collected in the employee portal.
  • The portal consent applies to the maximum extent permitted by law for employment-related background screening.
  • If state or local law requires additional stand-alone notices or authorizations, Progress Staffing Agency may provide them separately.
  • You should print or save a copy of this notice for your records.
