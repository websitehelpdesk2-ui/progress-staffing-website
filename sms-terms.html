SMS Terms & SMS Consent Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Program Description

Progress Staffing Agency ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") operates an SMS messaging program to communicate with registered employees, applicants, and client contacts regarding job assignments, shift updates, scheduling changes, onboarding requirements, portal notifications, and other employment-related matters.

2. Consent

By providing your mobile phone number during registration, application, or portal enrollment and by checking the applicable consent checkbox, you expressly consent to receive recurring automated text messages (SMS/MMS) from Progress Staffing Agency at the mobile number provided. Consent to receive text messages is not required as a condition of employment or any purchase.

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your enrollment status, shift activity, and account notifications. You may receive multiple messages per day during active assignment periods.

4. Message & Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan. Progress Staffing Agency is not responsible for any charges incurred by your mobile carrier.

5. Types of Messages Sent

  • Shift assignments and scheduling notifications
  • Shift cancellations or changes
  • Onboarding reminders and document requests
  • Timesheet approval alerts
  • Portal account activity (login, password changes)
  • General operational updates from your staffing coordinator

6. How to Opt Out

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us, or by updating your notification preferences in your employee portal under Account Settings. After opting out, you will receive a single confirmation message and no further messages will be sent unless you re-enroll.

7. How to Get Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us directly:

  • Email: info@progressstaffingagency.com
  • Phone: (725) 316-5242

8. Supported Carriers

SMS messaging is supported by most major US carriers including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and others. Carrier support is not guaranteed on all networks.

9. Privacy

Mobile phone numbers and SMS consent records are collected solely for employment communication purposes and will not be shared with third parties for marketing. For full details, review our Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this SMS Terms & Consent Policy at any time. Continued use of SMS communications after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
